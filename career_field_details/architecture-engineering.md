# Architecture & Engineering

## Overview
Architecture & Engineering focuses on designing, building, and improving the physical world. In Sri Lanka, it remains a prestigious and technically demanding field.

## Career paths
### Architect
**What they do:** Design buildings with attention to aesthetics, space, and functionality.  
**Path:** 5-year academic route leading to Chartered Architect status.

### Civil Engineer
**What they do:** Design and manage structures, infrastructure, drainage, and construction systems.  
**Path:** 4-year engineering degree leading to Chartered Engineer status.

### Biomedical Engineer
**What they do:** Work with medical technologies, devices, and hospital equipment.

## Where to study
### State universities
- University of Moratuwa
- University of Peradeniya
- University of Ruhuna
- University of Jaffna

### Professional and private institutes
- City School of Architecture
- SLIIT
- IESL
- NSBM Green University

## Entry requirements
- Engineering usually requires Maths stream and strong Z-score for state universities
- Architecture may require aptitude tests
- Private routes may accept broader A/L streams
- IESL offers a route without depending on Z-score

## Bridge courses
- AutoCAD / Revit
- Basic Surveying and Leveling
- Graphic Design software
- Maths and Physics refreshers
