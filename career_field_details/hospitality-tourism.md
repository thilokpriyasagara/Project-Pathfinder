# Hospitality & Tourism

## Overview
Hospitality & Tourism is a fast-growing industry in Sri Lanka, offering careers in hotels, travel, events, and food service. It is ideal for students who enjoy communication, service, and dynamic work environments.

## Career paths
### Hotel & Resort Management
**What they do:** Manage guest experience, front office, housekeeping, and food & beverage operations.

### Culinary Arts
**What they do:** Work as professional chefs in kitchens, restaurants, and hospitality settings.

### Travel & Event Management
**What they do:** Plan tours, events, conferences, and destination experiences.

## Where to study
### Government route
- SLITHM

### Private routes
- William Angliss @ SLIIT
- NSBM Green University
- Cinnamon Hospitality Academy

## Entry overview
- National Diploma at SLITHM usually requires 3 S passes in A/Ls and O/L English credit
- Degree routes usually accept 3 S passes in any stream
- Craft and certificate level courses may start from O/L qualifications

## Immediate steps
- Improve spoken English and other languages
- Follow a customer service or etiquette course
- Gain part-time event or service experience
- Build basic ICT skills
