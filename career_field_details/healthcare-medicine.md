# Healthcare & Medicine

## Overview
Healthcare & Medicine is one of the most respected and stable career sectors in Sri Lanka. It combines strong public-sector demand with growing private-sector and overseas opportunities.

## Career paths
### Surgeon / Doctor (MBBS)
**What they do:** Diagnose illnesses and treat patients, including through surgery.  
**Path:** 5-year MBBS + 1-year internship + specialization options.

### Nurse / Nurse Practitioner
**What they do:** Provide patient care, monitor recovery, administer treatment, and support doctors.  
**Career ladder:** Staff Nurse → Head Nurse → Matron → Nursing Administrator.

### Physiotherapist
**What they do:** Help patients recover movement and function after injury, illness, or surgery.

## Entry highlights
- Nursing schools generally require 3 S passes in A/Ls
- O/L credits commonly needed in Sinhala/Tamil, Mathematics, English, and Science
- Some private institutions have age and height requirements
- State medical and allied health degrees require strong Biology stream results and competitive Z-scores

## Immediate courses
- Foundation in Health Science
- Caregiving Certificate
- First Aid and BLS
- Pharmacy Technician courses

## Important note
Always verify whether the course is recognized by the relevant professional body such as SLMC or SLNC.
