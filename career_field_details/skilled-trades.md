# Skills-Based (Skilled Trades)

## Overview
Skills-Based Trades offer one of the fastest routes to employment and financial independence in Sri Lanka. The NVQ system makes these careers more professional, structured, and globally recognized.

## Career paths
### HVAC / Refrigeration Technician
**What they do:** Install and maintain cooling and air-conditioning systems.

### Electrician
**What they do:** Install and repair electrical systems in homes, businesses, and factories.

### Solar Technician
**What they do:** Specialize in solar power systems and related installations.

## NVQ pathway
- NVQ Level 3 — Basic Craftsman
- NVQ Level 4 — Full Craftsman
- NVQ Level 5/6 — Technician / Supervisor level
- NVQ Level 7 — Bachelor-level pathway

## Where to study
- German Tech (CGTTI)
- VTA
- NAITA

## Entry overview
- Electrician routes often require O/L passes including Mathematics
- HVAC routes can have lower minimum requirements
- Specialization pathways build on earlier NVQ levels

## Immediate steps
- Register at VTA or NAITA
- Prepare required safety gear
- Improve technical English
- Get a driving license if possible
