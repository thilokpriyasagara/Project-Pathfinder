# Business, Management & Administration

## Overview
Business, Management & Administration is a versatile field that supports every major industry. It is popular in Sri Lanka because students can combine degrees with professional qualifications and enter a wide range of careers.

## Career paths
### Human Resources Manager
**What they do:** Handle recruitment, training, employee relations, and workplace culture.  
**Key skills:** Communication, empathy, conflict resolution.

### Accountant / Financial Controller
**What they do:** Manage financial records, taxation, reporting, and strategic financial decisions.  
**Key skills:** Attention to detail, ethics, numerical reasoning.

### Business Development Manager
**What they do:** Drive company growth through partnerships, markets, and new customers.  
**Key skills:** Negotiation, strategic thinking, sales mindset.

- May 2026

### USJ internal degree
- Usually Commerce stream
- 3 S passes + Common General Paper minimum
- Admission depends heavily on Z-score

### USJ external degree
- 3 C passes in one sitting
- Must include at least one relevant subject such as Accounting, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics, or ICT

## Immediate steps
- Start CIMA or ACCA foundation
- Join CIPM Foundation if interested in HR
- Learn Microsoft Excel
- Improve Business English
