# Technology & Computing

## Overview
Technology & Computing is one of the most stable and high-paying career fields in Sri Lanka. As the country continues moving toward a digital economy, this field offers local and global job opportunities, including remote work and roles with foreign companies.

## Career paths
### Software Developer
**What they do:** Build applications, websites, mobile apps, and enterprise systems.  
**Key skills:** Logic, problem-solving, Python, Java, JavaScript.  
**Career ladder:** Trainee → Associate Software Engineer → Senior Engineer → Tech Lead.

### Cybersecurity Analyst
**What they do:** Protect systems, networks, and company data from cyber threats.  
**Key skills:** Networking, ethical hacking, threat analysis.  
**Career ladder:** SOC Analyst → Ethical Hacker → Security Architect.

### Data Scientist
**What they do:** Analyze data to find patterns, generate insights, and support decision-making.  
**Key skills:** Mathematics, Statistics, R, Python.  
**Career ladder:** Data Analyst → Data Scientist → AI Specialist.

## Entry routes
### UCSC BIT
- 3 S passes in any A/L stream in one sitting
- O/L: 6 passes with 3 credits including Mathematics and English
- FIT can be used as an alternative route

### SLIIT BSc IT
- 3 S passes in any stream
- Arts/Commerce students need O/L Mathematics C pass and IT Bridging Programme
- Must pass SLIIT Aptitude Test

### State university internal degrees
- Usually require Maths/ICT-related A/L background
- 3 S passes plus competitive Z-score
- Common General Paper minimum requirement applies
