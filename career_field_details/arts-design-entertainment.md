# Arts, Design & Entertainment

## Overview
Arts, Design & Entertainment is now a fast-growing professional sector in Sri Lanka, powered by digital media, branding, marketing, and content creation.

## Career paths
### Graphic Designer / Visual Communicator
**What they do:** Create visual content for brands, campaigns, interfaces, and communication materials.

### Fashion Designer
**What they do:** Design clothing, understand materials, and contribute to the apparel industry.

### Animator & VFX Artist
**What they do:** Produce motion graphics, 2D/3D animation, and visual effects.

## Where to study
### Specialized private schools
- AMDT School of Creativity
- Academy of Design (AOD)
- AIB

### State universities
- University of Moratuwa
- University of the Visual & Performing Arts
- University of Kelaniya

### Professional training
- SLIIT
- NIBM
- ESOFT

## Entry highlights
- AMDT generally accepts 2 A/L passes
- AOD typically requires 3 A/L passes plus portfolio/interview
- State design programs usually require high Z-score and aptitude tests

## Immediate steps
- Join a beginner short course
- Learn tools like Canva, Photoshop, Illustrator, Blender
- Build a portfolio
- Create a Behance or Instagram showcase

## Suggested CTA
Turn your creativity into a career by building skills and a strong portfolio.
