# Education

## Overview
Education is a respected and stable profession in Sri Lanka, with pathways into government schools, private institutions, and higher education.

## Career paths
### School Teacher
**What they do:** Teach, mentor, and guide students in classrooms.

### School Principal / Administrator
**What they do:** Lead schools, manage operations, staff, budgets, and policies.

### Lecturer / Professor
**What they do:** Teach in universities and contribute to academic research.

## Study and training routes
### National Colleges of Education (NCoE)
- 3-year National Diploma in Teaching
- Includes study plus internship
- Leads to government teaching opportunities

### State universities
- B.Ed. degrees at institutions such as University of Colombo and Open University

### Private institutes
- Horizon Campus
- SLIIT
- Lyceum Campus
- Gateway Graduate School
- St. George International Teacher Training Institute

## Key details
- NCoE admissions are usually announced through the Government Gazette
- Some private institutes offer trainee teacher and diploma pathways
- English-medium and international school pathways often value language proficiency and practical exposure

## Immediate steps
- TKT from British Council
- Diploma in Psychology and Counselling
- Montessori / Early Childhood training
- Volunteer tutoring
