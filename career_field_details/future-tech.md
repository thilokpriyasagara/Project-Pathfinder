# Future Tech

## Overview
Future Tech includes AI, Cloud, and Blockchain. It is one of the most globally connected career sectors and offers strong remote work and foreign-income opportunities.

## Career paths
### AI & Machine Learning Engineer
**What they do:** Build intelligent systems and data-driven prediction models.

### Cloud Architect
**What they do:** Design secure, scalable cloud systems using platforms such as AWS and Azure.

### Blockchain Developer
**What they do:** Build decentralized systems, smart contracts, and trusted digital platforms.

## Study options
- SLIIT AI specialization
- IIT Computer Science pathways in AI and Data Science
- UCSC Computer Science for strong research-focused route

## Professional certifications
- IIT Professional Certificate in AI
- SLIIT Advanced Certificate in AI
- AWS Cloud certifications

## Entry overview
- State AI routes usually require Maths stream and very high Z-score
- Private routes may accept broader streams with bridging support
- Cloud and blockchain certificates often require Math/English basics and programming knowledge

## Immediate steps
- Learn Python
- Complete entry-level cloud learning
- Explore Web3 and blockchain learning platforms
- Strengthen mathematics

Prepare for next-generation careers by building future-ready tech skills.
