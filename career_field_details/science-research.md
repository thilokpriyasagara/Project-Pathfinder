# Science & Research

## Overview
Science & Research is ideal for students who are curious, analytical, and interested in discovery. In Sri Lanka, this field supports industries such as healthcare, agriculture, environmental management, and advanced research.

## Career paths
### Microbiologist
**What they do:** Study microorganisms for healthcare, food safety, and agriculture.

### Physicist
**What they do:** Work in research, medical physics, telecommunications, and technology fields.

### Environmental Scientist
**What they do:** Study environmental problems and create solutions for sustainability, waste, and conservation.

## Where to study
### State universities
- University of Colombo
- University of Sri Jayewardenepura
- University of Peradeniya
- University of Kelaniya
- Open University of Sri Lanka

### External and professional routes
- External BSc programs through Colombo/UCSC-related pathways
- Amrak Institute
- NIFS

## Entry overview
- State internal BSc routes usually require 3 S passes in Science stream
- External and private routes may offer broader access
- Foundation programs can support O/L leavers

## Immediate steps
- Laboratory Technology Diploma
- Volunteer for citizen science or environmental groups
- Learn Python or R for scientific data analysis
- Improve Technical English

## Suggested CTA
Explore research-focused careers and build the scientific skills needed for future opportunities.
